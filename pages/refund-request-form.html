Our Refund Policy

5DollarTask aims to complete your task requests laboriously and diligently.

You can always request a refund for completed tasks, but only in situations where there are to-do items missed or not being worked on. We highly suggest reviewing the work being done by us after receiving the task-completion notification email.

You can also request a refund for a task not completed within 72 hours. This is regardless of whether there is a lack of communication from our end or your end.

Please note that we do not guarantee refund for tasks that are completed within 72 hours, specially, in situations where there are to-do items that were incorrectly done/made/processed due to a lack of clarification on your part.

You can only request a refund within 30 days after the task request has been created in our system.

Due to limitations of our system at the moment, refund requests can only be done by filling out the form below. Please note we do not guarantee refunds requested via email due to technical limitations.
